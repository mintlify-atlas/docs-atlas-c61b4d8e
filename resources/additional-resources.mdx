The tutorials in this collection are the heart of project-based learning, but they work best alongside a broader ecosystem of support. The resources below are supplemental platforms, communities, and blogs that complement hands-on project work — whether you need structured courses to fill knowledge gaps, coding challenges to sharpen fundamentals, or in-depth blog posts that explain the "why" behind the code you're writing. Bookmark the ones that match your learning style and return to them whenever a tutorial leaves you wanting more depth.
